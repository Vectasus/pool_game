Pool game

simple pool game in processing

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Design

Collision

Ball to ball

Condition

A ball hits a ball if the magnitude of the difference vector between the ball position vectors is smaller than the combined ball radii.

Result

Ball to wall

Condition

A ball hits a wall if: 1: the magnitude of the difference vector between the ball position vector and a wall vertex position vector is smaller than the ball radius. 2: the length of the line perpendicular to the line between two connected wall vertices is smaller than the ball radius.

Result

In both cases the balls velocity is multiplied

Ball to pocket

Condition

A ball hits a pocket if the magnitude of the difference vector between the ball position vector and the pocket position vector is smaller than the pocket radius.

Result
